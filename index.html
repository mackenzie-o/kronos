Time Dilation

def. The difference of elapsed time between two events as measured by observers either moving relative to each other or differently situated from a gravitational mass.

In other words, time dilation is the difference in the amount of time experienced between two locations that occurs when one party is moving faster (relative velocity time dilation) or is closer to an object of mass (gravitational time dilation). The faster the velocity difference between the two parties or the closer one party is to an object of mass, the more extreme effects of time dilation are.

Gravitational time dilation is caused by the deformation of the fabric of spacetime caused by objects of mass. Relative velocity time dilation is caused by ?spacetime too maybe?

So if you want to stay younger than your peers, your best bet is to get as close to the core of the earth as possible, and move as fast as possible.
