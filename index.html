About Time Dilation

Time dilation happens when things move REAL FAST or get REAL CLOSE to REAL BIG things. The FASTER or the BIGGER the thing the MORE time dilation occurs. Getting close to a BIG THING makes your time appear to go slower than someone's time who is further away from the BIG THING. Going REAL FAST makes your time appear to go slower than someone's time who is going LESS REAL FAST.

This happens because of reasons, but not enough that it matters to anyone but astronots and physicits and people who make GPS.
